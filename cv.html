Research Interests

My research focuses on making data pipelines more efficient by addressing critical bottlenecks in data science workflows. I develop systems for data discovery that enable users to find and join relevant datasets across large-scale data lakes through tabular interfaces and join path discovery. My research interests lie in data discovery, data wrangling, and data cleaning.
